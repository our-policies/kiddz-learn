Privacy Policy for Kiddz Learn

Effective Date: 23 September 2024

1. Introduction

At Kiddz Learn, we are committed to protecting the privacy of both parents and children who use our app. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information. As this app is designed for children under 8 years old, we follow strict guidelines to ensure compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable laws.

2. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Personal Information: We may collect limited personal information from parents or guardians during account creation or parental control setup, such as:
    • Kid's name
    • Email address
    • Payment information (for in-app purchases)
  • Non-Personal Information: We may collect non-personal information to improve the app experience, including:
    • Device type and operating system
    • Usage statistics (e.g., game scores, learning progress)
    • Anonymous identifiers (for analytics purposes)

3. How We Use the Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide a safe and engaging educational experience for children.
  • Allow parents to set up, monitor, and control their child’s app experience.
  • Improve app functionality, content, and user experience.
  • Communicate with parents regarding app updates, promotions, or support requests.

4. Parental Controls and Access

"Kiddz Learn" includes a parental access section that is protected by a password or PIN code. Only parents or guardians have access to:

  • Manage in-app settings.
  • Control in-app purchases.
  • Monitor their child's learning progress.
  • Set time limits for app usage.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for in-app purchases, analytics, or other functionalities. These services are required to follow privacy regulations to ensure the safety of your data. We do not share any personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

7. Children’s Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. Any data collected is under the supervision of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Parental Rights

  • Review any personal information collected about their child.
  • Request deletion of their child’s personal information.
  • Refuse further collection or use of their child’s information.
